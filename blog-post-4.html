Modular Kitchen Essentials

A beginner's guide to choosing the right layout, appliances, and finishes.

By Casa Chic Interior ‚Ä¢ August 5, 2025

1. Choosing the Right Layout

Popular layouts include L-shape, U-shape, and parallel kitchens. Choose based on your space and cooking habits. Compact spaces work best with straight or L-shaped kitchens, while larger areas can accommodate island counters.

2. Must-Have Appliances

Invest in built-in hobs, chimneys, microwaves, and dishwashers for a seamless look. Energy-efficient and smart appliances make life easier and enhance the kitchen‚Äôs modern appeal.

3. Modular Cabinets & Storage

Pull-out drawers, corner units, and tall cabinets are essentials in any modular setup. Use soft-close hinges and waterproof materials for long-term durability.

4. Materials & Finishes

Laminates, acrylic, glass, and PU finishes are trending. Go for matte textures in bold colors or classic white for a timeless look. Always choose heat- and water-resistant materials.

5. Lighting & Ventilation

Ensure proper task lighting over the cooking and washing areas. Use under-cabinet LED strips and natural ventilation wherever possible for a fresh cooking environment.
